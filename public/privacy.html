Privacy policy

This privacy policy stipulates the handling of user information in the smartphone application "Photo Record Diet Diary" and "Photo Record Diet Diary" provided by macho.blue.


1. Application provider name

macho.blue


2. User information handled by the application

When using this application, the following user information will be obtained via the application for the following purposes.

a. Record information

Acquisition method
User input (only when the user has backed up recorded information)
purpose of use
To back up recorded information for the purpose of transferring the recorded information to another terminal.

b. Advertising identifier

Acquisition method
Automatic acquisition
purpose of use
For advertising distribution suitable for users

3. External transmission / third party provision

a. Record information

External transmission

If the user makes a backup, it will be sent to the cloud storage provider.

Destination
Google Inc.
privacy policy
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Third party offer

There is no provision to third parties other than cloud storage providers.


b. Advertising identifier

External transmission

Sent to the advertising SDK provider.

Destination
Google Inc.
privacy policy
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Third party offer

There is no provision to third parties other than the advertising SDK provider.


4. Data Retention

a. Record information

Record information (photos, weight data, and diary entries) is stored locally on your device indefinitely until you delete it or uninstall the app.

If you create a backup:

  • Google Drive backup: Data is stored in your personal Google Drive account and retained according to your own Google Drive settings.
  • App server backup: Data is stored in Google Cloud Storage and automatically deleted after 2 weeks. This option is only available when you choose to backup to the app server instead of Google Drive.

b. Advertising identifier

Advertising identifiers are processed in real-time for ad delivery purposes and are not permanently stored by this application. Google may retain this information according to their own privacy policy.


5. Data Deletion

How to delete your data:

  • Local data: You can delete individual records within the app, or delete all local data by uninstalling the application.
  • Backup data:
    • Google Drive: You can delete backup data from your Google Drive account directly.
    • App server: Backup data stored in Google Cloud Storage is automatically deleted after 2 weeks. You can contact us at macho.macho.dev@gmail.com to request immediate deletion. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
  • Advertising data: You can reset your advertising identifier through your device settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID).

6. Methods of user involvement

By uninstalling this application by the user, the acquisition of user information will be stopped immediately.


7. Inquiries

Inquiries and consultations regarding the handling of user information in this application will be accepted at the following contact.

Contact information
macho.macho.dev@gmail.com

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

Due to the version upgrade of this application, the transmitted user information, purpose of use, etc. may be changed. Any changes will be announced on this site.
